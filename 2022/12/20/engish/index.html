六个学习英文的好习惯

Six good habits for learning English

1.设定短期(阶段性)目标

set phased objectives

目标遥远容易放弃

The goal is far away， it will be easy to give up.

定期检查自己是否达标，或者获得进步

Regularly check whether you meet the standards or make progress.

什么是阶段性目标

What are the milestones

eg: 去英文餐厅流利点餐

这样会学习很多和餐厅点餐的相关语句

目标要和自身相关，会有持续性有动力

The goal should be related to yourself, and it will be continuous and dynamic

明确自己缺乏哪些，可以主动学习

Identify what you lack and take the initiative to learn

学以致用

learn in order to practise

2.创造英文环境

Create an English environment

将日常生活很多东西变成英文

Turn many things in daily life into English

Eg: 看英文新闻

Watch the English news

如果困难，可以先查看中文，然后寻找对应的英文

If it is difficult, you can watch Chinese first and then find the corresponding English

娱乐变英文

make Entertainment to English

剧变成 英文

Watch English plays for entertainment

即便是娱乐的过程中，也可以为自己创造价值

Even in the process of entertainment, you can create value for yourself

接触的英文越频繁。越容易养成英文脑

The more frequent you are exposed to English. The easier it is to develop an English brain.

3. 建立正确的学习心态

Establish a correct learning attitude

不要好高骛远

Don’t aim too high

不要为了考试学英文

Don’t learn English for the exam.

不要因为英文进步水平有限，就苛责自己

Don’t scold yourself just because your English progress is limited

不要总觉得自己不会进步

Don’t always feel that you won’t make progress.

不要质疑自己的方法对不对

Don’t question your method, right?

自己行不行

Can you do it yourself

这样会让自己压力越来越大

This will make you more and more stressed

不要担心语法或者发音错误

Don’t worry about grammar or pronunciation mistakes

语言本身就是在错误中进行修正提升

Learning a language itself is to correct and promote mistakes

为了担心犯错而停止，才是影响自己进步的最大阻碍

Stopping for fear of making mistakes is the biggest obstacle to your progress

4. 寻找英语练习机会

Looking for English practice opportunities

找寻尽可能多的机会练习英语，去说，和老外对话

Look for as many opportunities as possible to practice English, talk and talk with foreigners

把英文当做工具使用

Use English as a tool.

5.运用零碎时间

5.Use spare time

比如 通勤时间，吃午饭时间

Such as commuting time, lunch time

尽可能去多的接触英语

Try to expose yourself with English as much as possible

6. 不要执着于单个单词

6. Don’t stick to single words

学会将单词 当做句子，考虑使用场景

Learn to use words as sentences and consider use scenarios

练习写作或者口语输出

Practice writing or oral output
